The Problem

Manual grading is costing you more than time.

Every exam cycle, institutions absorb the hidden cost of slow, error-prone, and exhausting OMR evaluation.

⏳
Hours lost per exam cycle

A single exam with 200 students can consume 2–4 full working days of staff time — time better spent on teaching and student support, not counting bubbles.

❌
Human errors are unavoidable

Fatigue, misaligned answer keys, and marking slip-ups introduce errors that take longer to find and correct than they would have taken to prevent entirely.

📦
Scale brings chaos

When hundreds of sheets arrive at once, the workflow collapses. Deadlines slip. Results are delayed. Institutions lose credibility with students and parents.

🗂️
Results trapped in paper

Compiling scores into usable records — for analysis, reporting, or student communication — adds yet another manual step that nobody has capacity for.

Features

Everything you need. Nothing you don't.

Every feature is designed for one goal — accurate exam results, as fast as possible.

Automated OMR Detection

SmartOMR finds and reads marked bubbles from any image — no manual alignment or special scanning required. Works with phone photos and flatbed scans alike.

Student ID Recognition

Each sheet is matched to a student via their bubble-filled ID number — ensuring results are always tied to the right person, without any manual entry.

QR-Based Exam Set Detection

Multiple exam sets in one session? SmartOMR reads the QR code on each sheet and automatically applies the correct answer key — no manual sorting required.

Instant Score Calculation

Scores are computed the moment a sheet is processed. Results stream live via the progress panel — no waiting, no batch delays, no manual tallying.

Batch Folder Processing

Upload an entire folder of images at once. SmartOMR processes every sheet in sequence — turning a full-day grading job into a task that finishes before your next meeting.

Manual Entry for Edge Cases

When a sheet is unreadable due to damage or poor image quality, the built-in manual entry modal lets you enter answers directly — so no student result is ever lost.

Excel Answer Key Upload

Upload your answer key as an Excel file (.xlsx). SmartOMR parses it automatically and applies it to every sheet in the session — supporting multiple exam sets.

Dual Excel Export

Export two reports per session: an Answer sheet with every student's selected answers per question, and a Result sheet with final scores — both in clean Excel format.

Secure Teacher Dashboard

Teachers log in with a username and PIN to access exam management. Create exams, manage answer keys, view all session reports, and control exam status — all in one dashboard.
