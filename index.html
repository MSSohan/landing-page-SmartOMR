FAQ

Quick answers to common questions

📱 Do I need a scanner?
Not at all. Just a smartphone. Snap the sheet, upload it, done. No hardware needed.
🎯 Can it handle bad lighting?
99%+ accuracy under normal conditions. Blurry or damaged sheets get flagged for manual entry.
⚡ How long does it take?
50 sheets in under 10 minutes. Results appear as each sheet finishes processing.
📊 Can I export to Excel?
Yes. One click. Answer exports with ID, set, and per-question data. Result exports with scores and pass/fail.
👨‍🏫 How many students per batch?
Unlimited. Drag in a folder with 200 sheets — it processes the whole batch in one go.
🔧 Is there any setup?
Zero. Open a browser, select your exam, snap a photo. That's it. No installs, no training.
